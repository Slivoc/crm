- SQLite-specific constructs to replace in early passes:
  * last_insert_rowid() -> INSERT ... RETURNING id (or cursor.fetchone()[0] pattern).
  * PRAGMA table_info and other PRAGMAs -> use migrations/schema or catalog queries.
  * INSERT OR IGNORE / INSERT OR REPLACE -> INSERT ... ON CONFLICT DO NOTHING/DO UPDATE.
  * datetime('now') and datetime(...) -> NOW() or parameterized timestamps.
  * '?' placeholders -> '%s' for psycopg2 (or adopt SQLAlchemy Core).
